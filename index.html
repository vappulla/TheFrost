About The Frost

The Frost provides a delightful experience with beautifully crafted dough-based desserts. Our commitment is to deliver high-quality, flavorful, and aesthetically pleasing treats, presented in a memorable way. The Frost is the creation of a self-taught and self-experienced individual who has refined their craft through dedication and experimentation. We are devoted to offering top-notch products suitable for all age groups, and we are truly grateful for all the support, love, and encouragement we have received.

Customization

You can personalize your order with custom cake toppers, printed message tags, and letter cards. Desserts and pastries can be customized with various designs, sizes, and flavors based on the options available in our menu. For themed celebration cakes, we also offer tailored themes, designs, and flavors in a range of sizes. For more information, please visit the respective pages on our website.

Available Flavors

All the flavors listed on our menu are available year-round, except for mango, which is seasonal.

Cake Ordering Details

  • Cakes are available both vegetarian and non-vegetarian
  • Orders for snack bites jar delicasies, and classic celebration cakes should be placed one in advance
  • For theme-based celebration cakes, please place your order two weeks in advance.
  • Can only be ordered online through Instagram or WhatsApp
  • Can be delivered through Dunzo, Porter, Swiggy(Genie)
  • Customization and price details can be discssed via WhatsApp after placing your order in the cart
